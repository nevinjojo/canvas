About Me

I am a software engineering intern at ANZ where I focus on developing the goMoney mobile banking application. I have a background in mobile development, web design, backend development, analysis, and testing. I enjoy collaborating with designers and developers in collectively building user-friendly interfaces.

Currently, I am teaching myself to program in Swift to develop an iOS budgeting app. I'm a self-proclaimed movie lover, who loves Wes Anderson films. I have a passion for traveling and an obsession over dogs. I am a programmer, an optimist, and a space enthusiast who's looking to take on the world one challenge at a time.

In addition to my development work, I am the co-founder and vice-president of the New Zealand Students' Space Association where we focus on empowering students who are passionate about being part of the New Zealand space scene.

When not online, I can be found at bootcamps and Meetup events, learning about the latest updates in the world of technology, or skating around the Wellington waterfront on a good day.
